How B5 influences scoring:

B5 affects only the Feasibility dimension, not Learning Design, Assessment Fit, Equity, or Stage Fit.

Apply soft adjustments only, e.g.:

Digital confidence

Low → small reduction to Online/HyFlex feasibility

High → small positive bump

Online teaching experience

None → reduce Online/HyFlex feasibility

Substantial → increase feasibility

Access to tech/HyFlex room

No HyFlex room → cap HyFlex feasibility

No digital tools → reduce Online feasibility

Workload complexity

3+ modules or heavy practical load → reduce feasibility of complex modalities

Light workload → neutral

These adjustments must be bounded, gentle, and cannot dominate the final recommendation.
